About us - SENTAMIZH SANGAM

Welcome to the website of the INDIAN INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY – HYDERABAD SENTAMIZH SANGAM. This sangam offers a platform for those who are interested in Tamil language and culture and also acts as a forum for the Tamil community at IITH. The sangam is the result of efforts by Tamil students who have been a part of IITH since its beginning in 2008. It took its present form on April 14, 2014, with the celebration of the Tamil New Year, bringing together faculty, staff, scholars and students. Sentamizh Sangam hopes to 1. Be a supportive platform for the Tamil students and alumni of IITH. 2. Encourage and enrich the learning of Tamil language. 3. Celebrate cultural festivals and open avenues for inter-cultural exchange. 4. Create informative tools for education in Tamil. This website is envisaged as a space to digitize, share, and promote our activities.
